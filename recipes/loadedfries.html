Ultimate Loaded Fries Recipe

Ingredients

FRIES

  • Potato chips
  • Salt
  • Parsley flakes

LOADED FRIES SAUCE

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Hot sauce
  • Kebab pepper
  • Parsley flakes

SEASONING MIX

  • Ginger powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Chilli pepper
  • Parsley flakes
  • All purpose seasoning
  • Bay leaf powder

OTHER INGREDIENTS

  • Minced meat
  • Chicken breast
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Create the seasoning mix by combining ginger powder, garlic powder, bay leaf powder, all-purpose seasoning, chili pepper (or red pepper), and parsley flakes.
  2. Make the loaded fries sauce by mixing mayonnaise, ketchup, hot sauce (or red chili sauce), mustard, kebab pepper, and parsley flakes. Use good quality condiments for best results.
  3. Season the chicken breast with the prepared seasoning mix and add oil to keep it moist and juicy.
  4. Marinate the chicken for 20-25 minutes (chicken breast doesn't need long marination).
  5. Cook the chicken in a pan with oil until done to your taste, or grill it in an air fryer.
  6. Heat oil in a saucepan and add onions for flavor.
  7. Add minced beef to the pan along with some of the seasoning mix and kebab pepper for spicy flavor.
  8. Stir-fry the minced meat until it's well-cooked, soft, and juicy.
  9. Deep fry the potato chips rather than shallow frying to prevent them from becoming too soft or burning quickly.
  10. Season the fried potatoes with parsley flakes and salt (salt is optional).
  11. Place the fried potatoes on your serving plate or bowl.
  12. Layer with cheese to help it melt when microwaved.
  13. Add the cooked chicken and minced beef on top of the fries.
  14. Generously apply the prepared sauce over the meat.
  15. Add more mustard if desired (honey mustard adds nice honey flavor).
  16. Sprinkle parsley flakes throughout (described as a "star ingredient").
  17. Top with more cheese if you want it extra cheesy (optional).
  18. Microwave for 1-3 minutes to melt the cheese.
  19. Finish with a final sprinkle of parsley flakes before serving.
